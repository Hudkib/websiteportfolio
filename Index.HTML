Nutrition Ninja

Social media graphics and content design for wellness brand

Project Overview

Nutrition Ninja is a wellness brand focused on providing accessible nutrition education through social media. The challenge was to create engaging, educational content that would stand out in crowded social feeds while maintaining a consistent brand identity.

I developed a comprehensive visual identity system including color palette, typography, and illustration style. The design approach focused on creating "snackable" content that could convey complex nutritional information in an easily digestible format.

The project included designing Instagram templates, infographics, story highlights, and a content strategy that increased engagement by 45% within the first three months.

Project Details

  • Client: Nutrition Ninja
  • Year: 2023
  • Services: Social Media Design, Brand Identity, Content Strategy
  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Social Media Page: instagram.com

Threeft Bazaar

E-commerce website design and complete brand identity

Project Overview

Threeft Bazaar is an online marketplace for unique, handcrafted goods from local artisans. The project required creating a complete brand identity and e-commerce website that would showcase the uniqueness of each product while providing a seamless shopping experience.

I developed a warm, inviting color palette and typography system that reflected the handmade nature of the products. The website design focused on creating visual hierarchy that highlighted product photography while maintaining intuitive navigation.

The project included logo design, brand guidelines, website UI/UX design, and marketing collateral. The new design resulted in a 30% increase in conversion rates and significantly reduced bounce rates.

Project Details

  • Client: Threeft Bazaar
  • Year: 2023
  • Services: Brand Identity, Web Design, UI/UX, E-commerce
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Webflow
  • Duration: 4 months
  • Website: threeft.co.ke

Dupont Paints

Corporate website design and digital presence

Project Overview

Dupont Paints is an established paint manufacturer looking to modernize their digital presence. The goal was to create a corporate website that reflected their industry leadership while making their extensive product catalog easily accessible to both trade professionals and consumers.

I designed a clean, professional interface with a focus on color visualization tools and product discovery. The design system incorporated their existing brand colors while introducing modern UI patterns that improved usability.

The project included website redesign, product catalog organization, color selector tool design, and responsive implementation. The new website improved user engagement metrics by 60% and significantly increased online inquiries from trade professionals.

Project Details

  • Client: Dupont Paints
  • Year: 2022
  • Services: Web Design, UI/UX, Corporate Branding
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, WordPress
  • Duration: 5 months
  • Website: dupontpaints.co.ke
